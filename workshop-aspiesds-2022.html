Workshop AspiesDS 2022

Workshops

In 2022 I did a fantastic workshop with autistic adults in Bern for Aspies Deutschschweiz. We learned the basics of sketchnoting and drew visuals about ourselves and our hobbies to get the hang out of it. In the second half of the workshop we got together and planned and realised a poster for an upcoming event of the organisation where non autistic people could ask autistic people questions. It was a very interesting afternoon.
