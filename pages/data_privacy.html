Data Privacy

You can contact me at sketchnotesara@gmail.com for any questions regarding your data.

What data do I collect?

We collect the following data:

  • Your email address if you choose to sign up to my newsletter. MailerLite is my newsletter provider. Email addresses are stored on their servers and according to their regulations. You can correct or delete your email address from our email list at any time using the link in any newsletter email or by contacting us directly. We will never shara your email address with others.
  • Our hosting provider may collect data related to your browsing behaviour such as your IP-address for security reasons. Our hosting provider is Github. This data is handled by them and according to their regulations, we ourselves don't have access to this data.
  • We only use strictly necessary cookies on our website.

Privacy policies of other websites

Our website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.
