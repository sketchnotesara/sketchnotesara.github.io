About

I once was a photonics engineer, who, after being diagnosed autistic, changed career to become a software developer. I love creating things, be it writing software for different projects, sewing my own clothes in my free time, or drawing visual notes for everything. I implement illustration as a form of communication and understanding rather than as a fine art, drawing from experience, texts, or presentations. Born and raised in Belgium, I now live in Switzerland, where I love exploring the beautiful nature.

I speak German, English and Dutch fluently, as well as French and Turkish on an intermediate level.
