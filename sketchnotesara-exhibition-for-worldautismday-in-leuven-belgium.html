Sketchnotesara exhibition for Worldautismday in Leuven (Belgium)

Autistic Life Comics

From March 28, 2023 until April 10, 2023 the very first exhibition of my work took place at the KULeuven. Many of my drawings on being autistic were shown, some in Dutch, some in English. The opening event on March 28 also featured the showing of the documentary Seeing the unseen, about autistic women.
