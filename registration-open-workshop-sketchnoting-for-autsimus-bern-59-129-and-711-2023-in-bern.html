(Registration open) Workshop sketchnoting for Autsimus Bern 5.9, 12.9 and 7.11 2023 in Bern

Workshops

In autumn 2023 I will be giving a 3 evening workshop Sketchnoting for Autismus Bern. On the first day of the workshop, I will introduce how I use sketchnoting as an autistic adult, and participants will draw along while I introduce the basics of sketchnoting. On the second and third evening of the workshop we will work on our own visualisation projects around autism and communication, with my personal help and feedback. The workshop will be in German.
