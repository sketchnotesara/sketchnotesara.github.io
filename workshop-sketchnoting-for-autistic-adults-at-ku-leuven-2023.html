Workshop sketchnoting for autistic adults at KU Leuven 2023

Workshops

In March 2023 I got the oportunity to do a workshop for autistic adults again. This time it took place at the KULeuven in Belgium. In the first part of the workshop, participants drew along while I introduced the basics of sketchnoting. In the second half of the workshop we drew about our experiences being autistic. I admired the creativity and gave personal feedback during the drawing process. In the end everyone had at least one beautiful drawing to present. Some of the results were featured in the exhibition that started the next day.

Sara Peeters
