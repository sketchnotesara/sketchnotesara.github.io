Autistic Life Comics

I draw about being autistic and I love it. This project started out as some sketches not meant to be seen by anyone, but became a huge succes with autistic an non-autistic people as soon as I started sharing it. I have an ever growing list of things I still want to draw about. You can follow my autistic life comics on social media, or subscribe to my newsletter and get them straight to your inbox.
