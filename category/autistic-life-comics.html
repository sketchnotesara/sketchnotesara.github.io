Autistic Life Comics

I draw about being autistic and I love it. This project started out as some sketches not meant to be seen by anyone, but became a huge succes with autistic an non-autistic people as soon as I started sharing it. I have an ever growing list of things I still want to draw about. You can follow my autistic life comics on social media, or you can browse them directly here on my website, and subscribe to my newsletter to receive updates to your inbox.
