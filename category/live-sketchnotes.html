Live Sketchnotes

Live Sketchnotes are made in real time at your event. I draw while the presenter is speaking, and when the presentation is finished, the sketchnote is ready as well. I work on paper to make live sketchnotes, but digitalisation after the event is possible as well.

Note: I don't do live sketchnotes at the moment. If you want me to make a sketchnote of existing content instead, don't hesitate to get in touch.
