Illustration

Sometimes it is a fine line between sketchnotes and illustration. Because I always draw with the intention to get a message across. Is it a little bit less text, the message being more of an atmosphere than tangible content, or is there really no substantial difference?

Besides sketchnotes that can be used in presentations or printed publications, I also make drawings that can serve as a logo or can be printed on t-shirts. I've made illustrations for books, associations and conferences. If you like my style, we can work together!
