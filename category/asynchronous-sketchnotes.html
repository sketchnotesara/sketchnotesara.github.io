Asynchronous Sketchnotes

Asynchronous sketchnotes are drawings I make from behind my desk, of pre-recorded videos or written content. If you have ideas about what the sketchnote should look like, we can discuss those, or I can generate a first version and we can take it from there. As those sketchnotes are not made in real time, there is more room to incorporate feedback.

An asynchronous sketchnote or visualisation can give any content more power, on social media, on your website or printed publication, or in a presentation. Depending on de intended use we can optimize the format or make multiple versions.
