Live Sketchnotes at Autismusdialog 2022 in Bern

Live Sketchnotes

The Autismusdialog is an anual format of Autismus Bern around a topic related to autism. The theme of 2022 was food and eating.

There were 3 presentations which I drew on A3 paper, photographed immediately after the presentation and showed to the crowd during the Q&A. At the end of the Q&A, I quickly walked the participants through what I had drawn. After the conference, I digitized the talk drawings, so that they could be easily distributed to the participants.
