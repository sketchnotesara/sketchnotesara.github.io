Learning from autistic Teachers Book Illustration

Illustration

I was asked to make an illustration for the book Learning from autistic Teachers edited by Rebecca Woods. To accomplish this I read the manuscript and made a small illustration representing each chapter. I than put everything together to one big image for the book.

I wrote about my process and the content of the book on linkedIn.
